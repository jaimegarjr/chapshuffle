Chap Shuffle

Thanks for installing!

Chap Shuffle adds a shuffle button directly to the YouTube player. On any video with 5 or more chapters, click it to randomize the queue and auto-advance through chapters like tracks on an album — great for soundtracks, long-form mixes, compilations, and anything where the chapters stand on their own.

Get started

  1. 1 Open a YouTube video that has chapters — look for timestamps in the description or chapter markers on the progress bar.
  2. 2 Click the ⇄ shuffle icon that appears in the player controls bar.
  3. 3 The queue opens with chapters in random order. Hit play and let it run, or navigate manually with the arrow buttons.
